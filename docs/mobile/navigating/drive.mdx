# Drive

# Here you have a direct overview of all your cloud folders.

You can view the drive either as a **list** or as a **grid**.  
Simply click on the 3 dots in the top right corner, and select View as:

-   List
-   Grid

![Home](/img/mobilelistgrid.png)

Right next to it, you can click on the plus symbol and you will have the following options:

![Home](/img/mobileadd.png)

Furthermore, you can view **active transfers** there or **mark items** for further **actions** such as **moving**.

![Home](/img/mobiledrivemisc.png)

Press and hold a file or folder to open the action window shown above.

-   **Select**  
    Here you can select the corresponding item again for further actions.
-   **Open**  
    This simply opens the selected folder or file directly
-   **Share**  
    Here you can share the items with your contacts
-   **Favorite**
    Press the heart and mark your files and folders as favorites to quicly access them in the home screen
-   **Info**  
    This will show you information about the file or filder such as:

    -   Name
    -   Size
    -   Directories (amount)
    -   Files (amount)
    -   Type
    -   Location (the location path in the cloud)
    -   modified (time & date)
    -   uploaded (time & date of the upload)
