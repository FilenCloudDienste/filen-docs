# Logging into the Filen Mobile App

As soon as you have downloaded and installed the appropriate mobile app, you will be greeted with the login screen.  
(Here you also have the option of creating a free Filen account above the "Log in" button.)

Simply enter your previously selected login information and click on **Log in**.
