# Download

Open your preferred web browser.

-   Head over to our [website](https://filen.io/products/desktop)
-   On the top left, click on "Products"
-   Click on "Desktop"

Now scroll down until you see 3 tabs, Windows / macOS / Linux

You can also download the following applications directly by clicking on the corresponding link:
