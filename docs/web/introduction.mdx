# Introduction

To get started, you need an activated [Filen account](https://app.filen.io/#/register).  
(Filen accounts are always free and offer 10GB of storage space)

In this guide, we'll show you how to get started with Filen  
and how make the most of our features for storing, sharing, and managing your files.
