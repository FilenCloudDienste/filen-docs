# Logging into your Filen account

Open your preferred web browser.

-   Go to the [Filen.io website](https://filen.io/)
-   Click on the "Log In" or "Create an account" button.
-   Enter your Filen E-Mail and password or create a new account.
-   Click the "Log In" button to access your account.

The site will ask you about your cookie preferences.  
Choose whether you agree to allow cookies or only essential cookies.  
**We use these cookies to improve the user experience on our site, no personal data is collected**
