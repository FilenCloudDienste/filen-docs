# Settings and Security

Explore your account settings to customize your Filen experience.  
To get to your settings, click on the round image in the bottom left corner of your web drive.  
There you can choose between various tabs.

**Here are frequently used functions and where you can find them:**

-   **Change your profile picture.**
    -   [Account](https://app.filen.io/#/settings/general) > Avatar > Edit > Upload picture
-   **Change your email.**
    -   [Account](https://app.filen.io/#/settings/general) > Email address > Edit
-   **Choose a name for your chat instead of your email**
    -   [Account](https://app.filen.io/#/settings/general) > Chat Settings > Edit
-   **Switch to Dark/Light Mode**
    -   [General](https://app.filen.io/#/settings/general) > Theme > Dark/White/System
-   **Change the language**
    -   [General](https://app.filen.io/#/settings/general) > Language > Click on preferred language
-   **Customize your invoice by personalizing it**
    -   [Account](https://app.filen.io/#/settings/account) > Personal Information > Edit
-   **Find out what we save about you**
    -   [Account](https://app.filen.io/#/settings/account) > Request account data > Request
-   **Delete versioned files**
    -   [Account](https://app.filen.io/#/settings/account) > Versioned Files > Delete
-   **Disable file versioning**
    -   [Account](https://app.filen.io/#/settings/account) > File Versioning > Turn Slider Off
-   **Enable login alerts**
    -   [Account](https://app.filen.io/#/settings/account) > Login Alerts > Turn Slider on
-   **Delete your account**
    -   [Account](https://app.filen.io/#/settings/account) > Request account Deletion > Request
-   **Enable two-factor authentication (2FA)**
    -   [Security](https://app.filen.io/#/settings/security) > Two Factor Authentication > Turn Slider On
-   **Change your password**
    -   [Security](https://app.filen.io/#/settings/security) > Change Password > Change
-   **Export your master keys (_important_)**
    -   [Security](https://app.filen.io/#/settings/security) > Export Master Keys > Export
-   **Purchase more storage**
    -   [Plans](https://app.filen.io/#/settings/plans) > Choose your plan > Buy now
-   **See your current plan**
    -   [Subscriptions](https://app.filen.io/#/settings/subscriptions) > All active subscriptions will be shown here
-   **View and download your invoices**
    -   [Invoices](https://app.filen.io/#/settings/invoices) > All current and past invoices will be shown here
-   **View your account events**
    -   [Events](https://app.filen.io/#/settings/events) > Click on Event > See Device, Date Name and more.

**Logout**

To log out, click your profile picture or setting icon in the bottom left corner.
Select "Log Out" in the bottom left corner to end your session securely.
